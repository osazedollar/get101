How It Works

Sopra is a platform that connects users with local service providers for on-demand delivery of goods and services.

  • 01 Place an Order

    Users can place orders through the app, specifying their requirements, and nearby service providers receive notifications to fulfill those orders.

  • 02 Accept or Reject Orders

    Service Providers can Accept or Reject orders based on their availabilty and capacity Once Accepted, they proceed to fulfill the order.

  • 03 Tracking Capability

    Users can track the delivery in real-time through the app.

FAQ

What is Sopra

Sopra is an on-demand delivery service application that connects users with local delivery providers to facilitate quick and convenient delivery of various items.


What can i delivery using Sopra

Sopra can be used to deliver a wide range of items, including groceries, food orders from restaurants, packages, documents, and more. As long as it's legal and safe to transport, Sopra can handle it.


How can I become a delivery provider for Sopra?

If you're interested in becoming a delivery provider for Sopra, you can sign up through the app or website. Once approved, you can start receiving delivery requests and earning income by fulfilling them.
